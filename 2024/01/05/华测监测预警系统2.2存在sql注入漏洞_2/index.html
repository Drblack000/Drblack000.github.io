Vulnerability Description

CTI Monitoring Integration Division makes full use of its technological accumulation in geological disaster monitoring to establish a scientific and complete geological disaster monitoring and early warning platform, realizing scientific, informatization, standardization and visualization of geological disaster prevention and control management. The product version 2.2 url:/Handler/Ajax.ashx?oper=getProjectAllInfo, the ProjectId parameter in the cookie has a sql injection vulnerability.
